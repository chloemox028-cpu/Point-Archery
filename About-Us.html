History

Straight to the Point Archery: Mission, History, Facilities, and Policies

Founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen, Straight to the Point Archery is a non-profit archery education center dedicated to teaching archery to individuals of all ages and abilities.

Mission:

To provide a welcoming environment for archery education, promoting valuable life skills and inclusivity for all students.

Policies

The center adheres to a nondiscriminatory policy, admitting students of any race, gender, sexuality, religion, or national origin, ensuring equal access to all programs and activities.

Our Facilities:

The center features an indoor training facility with 28 shooting lanes (14 ten-yard and 14 twenty-yard), 2 private coaching rooms, and access to additional outdoor shooting fields.

Nondiscriminatory Policy

Straight to the Point Archery admits students of any race, gender presentation, sexuality, religion, national origin, or ethnic origin to all rights, privileges, programs, and activities available to students at the school. We do not discriminate in administering our educational policies, admission policies, and other school-administered programs. All students are welcome and valuable members of our community.

We offer:

Several lessons that accomodate everyone from first-time archers to professional archers.

Note:

Once set up with properly fitting equipment, each archer receives basic safety instruction and is introduced to various shooting techniques.
