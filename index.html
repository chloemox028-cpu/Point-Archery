History

Straight to the Point Archery

A non-profit archery education center founded in 2005 by Giap Nguyen.Our state-of-the-art educational and training facility combines detailed technical video analysis with generations of archery experience from certified coaches and instructors.We have excellent connections with USA Archery (USAA), Washington State Archery Assocation (WSAA), Washington State Bowhunters (WSB), National Field Archery Assocaiation (NFAA), and manymore national and many more national and state archery associations. These partnerships allow you to feel confident in our facility and staff.

Our Facilities:

We are indoor training facility with

  • 14 ten-yard lanes
  • 14 twenty yard lanes
  • 2 private coaching rooms

Nondiscriminatory Policy

Straight to the Point Archery admits students of any race, gender presentation, sexuality, religion, national origin, or ethnic origin to all rights, privileges, programs, and activities available to students at the school. We do not discriminate in administering our educational policies, admission policies, and other school-administered programs. All students are welcome and valuable members of our community.

We offer:

Several lessons that accomodate everyone from first-time archers to professional archers.

Note:

Once set up with properly fitting equipment, each archer receives basic safety instruction and is introduced to various shooting techniques.
