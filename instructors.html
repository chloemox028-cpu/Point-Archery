Our instructors are qualified Level 1 and Level 2 coaches who have met the safety requirements of USA Archery (USAA). We teach in a positive learning environment. Our instructors also have extensive knowledge of the most advanced archery equipment and all forms of archery, from the traditional longbow to the modern compound bow. We have specific instructors for recreational archery, competitive archery, and bow hunting.

Our staff has decades of experience in the archery industry. We are committed to educating and assisting with any questions you may have.

Nathan is a USA Archery Certified Level 3 NTS Coach and has been teaching archery since 2009. He developed an interest in archery when he nocked his first arrow as a Boy Scout at camp. Nathan has many years of experience teaching people of all ages, gained as a martial arts instructor and studio manager

Derek is a Washington native and an experienced outdoorsman with many years of hunting, fishing, skiing, canoeing, archery, shooting, and minimalist camping. His mastery of bushcraft and wilderness survival knowledge defines his character. He enjoys handcrafting his own longbows, flint-knapped primitive arrows, and making custom leather products.

Amy truly has a passion for archery. She comes from a long line of hunters, fishermen, and trappers in Canada. Amy places a strong emphasis on safety and is enthusiastic about teaching. She also loves fitness and has been a YMCA group exercise and personal trainer for over 18 years.
